Current

In current times web related rescources are becoming more and more common, gone our the days of "W3schools" and their divs upon divs of content, and in with the new such as "Mozilla Developer Network" or "CSS Tricks".

We have moved away from using divs in most places and have instead moved to semantic element tags instead, this means instead of using a div tag for a header, we now use a header tag. this allows for ease of communication between the people making a website.
